Cookies

Cookies are not used to identify individuals - only demographic groups. We use cookies when you visit our site to give you a better experience and to analyse our website traffic. You can choose to not allow some cookies but this may impact your experience on the site.

What are cookies?

Cookies are data files which can hold small amounts of info and they are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, etc) when you first visit a website.

How we use cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Site navigation
  • Allowing you to add items to your basket
  • Analysing users and their behaviours on the site
  • Helping us determine whether you came to nodcaps from a particular Internet link or banner advertisement.
  • Helping us m easure optimize and build audiences for your ad campaigns.
  • Identifying returning users

When someone visits accepts our analytics cookies, we use Google Analytics to collect standard internet log information and details of visitor behaviour patterns. We do this to find out things such as the number of visitors to the various parts of the site. This information is only processed in a way which does not identify anyone

When someone visits accepts our targeting cookies, we use meta pixel tags to add a snippet of JavaScript code that allows us to track visitor activity on the website. We use thos to measure the effectiveness of ads, to define custom audiences for ad targeting, for Advantage+ catalog ads campaigns, and to analyze that effectiveness of our website's conversion funnels.
